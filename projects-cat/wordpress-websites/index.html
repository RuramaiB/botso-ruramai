As a front-end developer for the Edelweiss Online Store, I played a crucial role in bringing the vision of the online shopping experience to life. My primary responsibility was to translate design mockups and wireframes into responsive web pages using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I focused on creating a seamless and intuitive user experience that would engage customers and encourage them to explore the store.

In addition to UI/UX design implementation, I adopted a mobile-first design approach to ensure that the online store was fully functional and visually appealing across various devices and screen sizes. This involved creating reusable UI components and libraries, which not only enhanced consistency in the application but also streamlined the development process for future features.

Collaboration was key in my role, as I worked closely with back-end developers to integrate APIs for dynamic content loading. This ensured a smooth data flow and functionality within the application. I also took the initiative to conduct thorough testing and debugging across different browsers and devices, guaranteeing that the online store performed consistently for all users.
